PETLI

Petli is an app where you can train your dog, read articles and connect with other dog lovers. I started working with them in 2021 when I designed icons for them and edited them into the 500-something training videos that are on the app. The banners show the old version of the app when it was still free, and the videos are from 2023 marketing the app on different social media platforms. The videos were made in After Effects and music was added by me from Epidemic sound.
